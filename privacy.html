Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Your privacy matters. This page explains what data Creator Earnings Calculator collects (spoiler: almost none) and how it's handled.

1. No account required

You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use this tool. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

2. Data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the values you enter into the calculator. Your slider positions, RPM inputs, and tax settings never leave your device.

3. Cookies & local storage

We may store your theme preference (dark/light mode) locally in your browser. No tracking cookies or cross-site identifiers are used.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly, anonymised analytics (such as page view counts) to understand how the tool is used in aggregate. This data cannot be used to identify you.

5. Third-party services

If you choose to support the project via the donate link, you will be directed to a third-party payment processor with its own privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out via the Support link in the app.
