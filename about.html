About Us

Creator Earnings Calculator

Creator Earnings Calculator is a free tool built to help streamers, YouTubers, and content creators get a realistic picture of their potential income — from ad revenue and subscriptions to sponsorships and tips.

What we do

We aggregate publicly available RPM and CPM data across platforms like Twitch, Kick, and YouTube, and combine it with real-world revenue stack scenarios so you can model your earnings before and after tax.

Why we built it

Most income estimates floating around the internet are vague or cherry-picked. We wanted a tool that's honest, transparent about its assumptions, and actually useful for planning — whether you're just starting out or scaling up.

Accuracy disclaimer

All figures are estimates based on mid-range industry data and are for informational purposes only. Actual earnings vary based on niche, audience geography, engagement, platform algorithm changes, and many other factors.
